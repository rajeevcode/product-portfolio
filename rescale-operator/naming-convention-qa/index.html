Naming Convention QA Agent

Rules + AI validation for campaign data quality — parses naming fields, classifies valid / partial / invalid patterns, and routes exceptions for human review.

What it does

Ingests campaign and ad names, extracts structured tokens (product, market, creative type, etc.), applies brand-specific rules from config, and surfaces QA status in the intelligence dashboard.
