The Problem: Marketplace Operations Needed Better Visibility, Governance, and Customer Workflow Automation

Jumia operated across multiple markets with complex marketplace workflows, customer journeys, support operations, and executive reporting needs. Teams needed better governed dashboards, data lineage, reliable refresh SLAs, localized tracking, proactive customer communication, and automation to reduce support workload.

Fragmented marketplace reporting

Reporting was scattered, making consistent decisions hard.

Limited data lineage

It was unclear where numbers came from, undermining trust.

Dashboard refresh reliability issues

Dashboards refreshed inconsistently, eroding confidence.

High support workload

Support teams carried heavy, avoidable contact volume.

Localized order tracking gaps

Order status was not consistently localized for customers.

Reactive customer communication

Customers were updated late, after issues had already surfaced.

Multi-market operational complexity

Each market added operational and reporting complexity.

Limited executive visibility

Leadership lacked reliable, consolidated operating visibility.

Solution / product approach

A layered flow turning marketplace data into governed visibility, proactive customer communication, and operating savings.

  1. Marketplace / order / customer / regional data — the raw inputs.
  2. ↓ Data governance + lineage — trustworthy, traceable data.
  3. ↓ Dashboard refresh SLAs — reliable, on-time reporting.
  4. ↓ Executive reporting — consolidated leadership visibility.
  5. ↓ Localized order tracking — clear status for customers.
  6. ↓ Proactive notifications — update customers before they ask.
  7. ↓ Support workflow automation — reduce avoidable contacts.
  8. ↓ Marketplace operating savings — the measured outcome.
