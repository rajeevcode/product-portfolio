The Problem: Marketplace Growth Needed Reliable Payment APIs, Better Checkout, and Localized APAC Workflows

Lazada needed scalable marketplace growth across APAC markets. The challenge involved standardizing payment APIs, integrating wallets, improving checkout controls, increasing transaction success, reducing friction, and adapting workflows to regional market needs.

Payment API fragmentation

Payment integrations varied, making the platform hard to scale.

Wallet integration complexity

Integrating diverse wallets across markets was complex.

Checkout friction

Checkout friction cost conversions at the critical moment.

Transaction success gaps

Transactions failed inconsistently across markets and partners.

Cross-market localization challenges

Workflows needed adapting to each market's needs.

Discovery conversion issues

Discovery did not consistently convert browsing into purchase.

Payment partner coordination

Coordinating many payment partners added operational overhead.

Platform workflow inconsistency

Inconsistent workflows made the platform harder to grow.

Solution / product approach

A layered flow turning fragmented payment and checkout inputs into scalable APAC marketplace growth.

  1. Customer / seller / payment partner inputs, the raw inputs.
  2. ↓ Standardized payment APIs, one consistent integration model.
  3. ↓ Wallet integrations, diverse wallets connected reliably.
  4. ↓ Checkout controls, reduce friction and protect success.
  5. ↓ Localized APAC workflows, adapt to each market.
  6. ↓ Discovery & recommendation improvements, convert browsing to purchase.
  7. ↓ Conversion optimization, lift checkout and discovery conversion.
  8. ↓ Scalable marketplace growth, the measured outcome.
